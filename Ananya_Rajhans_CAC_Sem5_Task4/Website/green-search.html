The gender wage gap refers to the difference between the average earnings of men and women in the workforce. It is typically expressed as a percentage of men's earnings, highlighting the disparity in income between genders. This gap exists across various industries, occupations, and educational levels and is influenced by a combination of factors such as discrimination, occupational segregation, differences in work experience, and societal expectations. The gender wage gap is a critical issue as it reflects and perpetuates gender inequality, limiting economic opportunities for women and contributing to broader socio-economic disparities.
